Founder Profile

A leader at the intersection of security, technology & business.

Pascal Ng'andu is the founder of ITCore Technologies, a professional IT and cybersecurity brand based in Lusaka, Zambia. With a background in Information Security, Computer Forensics, and Business Administration, Pascal brings together technical expertise, digital investigation knowledge, and business-focused strategy.

His vision for ITCore is to help individuals, businesses, churches, and organizations build secure, reliable, and modern digital systems. Through cybersecurity, IT consulting, computer forensics, networking, and business technology support, Pascal leads ITCore with integrity, excellence, and a commitment to international standards.
